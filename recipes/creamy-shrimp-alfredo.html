Cajun Shrimp Alfredo





Description

This creamy and spicy concoction will have your tastebuds excited.

This is spicy twist on your typical shrimp alfredo. Some parmesan will put this recipe over the top!



Ingredients

  • ½ pound andouille sausage, diced
  • ¼ onion, diced
  • ¼ cup diced celery
  • ¼ cup diced red bell pepper
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
  • ¼ teaspoon dried sage
  • 1 pound raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • 1 (1.6 oz) package dry Alfredo sauce mix (such as Knorr®)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic, divided
